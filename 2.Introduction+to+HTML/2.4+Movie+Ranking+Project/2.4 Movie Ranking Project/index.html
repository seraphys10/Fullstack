The Best Movies According to Seraphys

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Forrest Gump

This film delivers a heartfelt story that blends humor, drama, and history. Forrest’s simple yet profound perspective on life offers inspiring lessons about love, perseverance, and destiny.

The Matrix

It revolutionized science fiction with groundbreaking visual effects and a thought-provoking story about reality, choice, and human freedom. The philosophical depth and action sequences still feel fresh today.

Titanic

This epic romance combines historical tragedy with an emotional love story, brought to life by stunning cinematography and a memorable score. It’s both visually breathtaking and emotionally moving.
